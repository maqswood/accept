MENU CARD

A menu card contains information about a list of food, beverages,
and other items available for order. It can also provide details
about the restaurant's branding and theme.

E-POSTER

An e-poster is a poster in PowerPoint format that can include multimedia
elements like videos, images, and audio clips. E-posters are displayed
on large monitors or computers.

BROCHURE

A brochure is an informative paper document that's often used to share
promotional material. Brochures can vary in content and length, but most
follow a standard format.

Motion Graphics

Motion graphics are pieces of animation or digital footage that create the
illusion of motion or rotation. They are often combined with audio for use in multimedia projects.

Motion Poster

A Moster, or motion poster, is a high resolution animation of an original film poster
authorized by the movie's film studio. The concept was developed and the term coined by
GeekNation.com. A Moster was designed for Hard Candy, and released on GeekNation.com.
